Export to Excel

 

 

 

 

Forecast results can be exported to Excel.

From Precept Main User Interface, Click on "Export to Excel"

 - Select the name of the scenario to be exported (one or several scenario can be exported at the same time).

 - Select the Nodes of interest (All Nodes, or you cna export for the field only, or only selected nodes or wells).

 - Select the reporting frequency (Daily, Monthly or Yearly)

 - Click "Export Forecast to Excel"

 

Custom Reporting

Cleverfield can also provide Custom reporting in custom Excel format, or export forecast results in a separate database.

Contact Cleverfield support@cleverfield.fr for more informations.
